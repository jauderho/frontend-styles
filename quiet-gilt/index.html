The ledger

Restraint is the whole discipline.

The rarest skill in managing a fortune is the patience to do almost nothing. Three principles govern every decision we make on your behalf.

01

Restraint

We do less, deliberately. Most damage to capital is self-inflicted — the cost of acting when stillness would serve better.

02

Permanence

We invest on a generational horizon. Our average holding outlives the funds that chase it, and most of the people who run them.

03

Discretion

Your name appears nowhere. Privacy is not a service we offer; it is the foundation the house is built upon.
